About NightShare

The most stylish and secure way to share your files online.

Our Mission

NightShare was created with a simple goal: to make file sharing beautiful, intuitive, and secure. We believe that sharing your important files shouldn't be complicated or boring.

Our platform combines sleek design with powerful functionality, giving you complete control over your content while making the experience enjoyable.

Key Features

Privacy Controls

Choose between private, unlisted, or public sharing options for each file you upload.

Fast Transfers

Upload and download files at lightning speed with our optimized transfer technology.

Secure Storage

All files are encrypted and stored securely on our protected cloud infrastructure.

Frequently Asked Questions

What file types can I upload?

You can upload almost any file type to NightShare, including images, videos, documents, archives, and more.

How long are my files stored?

Free accounts can store files for up to 30 days. Premium accounts have unlimited storage time.

What's the maximum file size?

Free accounts can upload files up to 500MB. Premium accounts can upload files up to 10GB.

How secure is NightShare?

We use industry-standard encryption for all files and transfers. Your private files can only be accessed by you or those you share links with.
